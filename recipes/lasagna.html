Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish originating from the region of Sicilly. With it's humble origins, it has become a worldwide favorite in homes all over.

Lasagna is made by layering a sauce, typically made of meat, herbs and tomatoes, cheese, primarily Mozerella and Ricotta, and sheets of boiled pasta. Thedish in assembled in a tray, topped with a generous helping of grated parmesan before being baked i the oven.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Sausage
  • Mozerella
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Take sausage and cook as a crumble.
  2. Drain fat and set aside.
  3. Take a large sauce pan and add herbs and simmer. Add the sauage and let on low heat to let water evaporate.
  4. After sauce reaches desired thickness, take lasagna pan and spread sauce on the bottom. Add cheese then pasta and continuie layering. finish by topping with sauce, cheese and finally grated parmesan.
  5. Put in oven and bake for about 25 minutes.
